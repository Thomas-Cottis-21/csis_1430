About me

Hey there! My name name is Thomas Joseph Cottis and I am currently working as a delivery driver for a medical equipment distributor by day, and a web-developer freelancing by night.

I love to travel, learn new things, create things and build aesthetic things. For example, I am really into art, interior design, photography, movies, music and pretty much anything else that any other young person is into (besides drugs lol)

I served a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Lima, Peru from 2018 to 2020. Before that I spent 3 weeks in the Dominican Republic building a small school with a service group and I absolutely loved it. I would recommend to everyone to go and see the world, but as you do to make sure that you make wherever you go a better place.

How to spend time wisely on the web

Here is a list of the top ten things that I watch out for when I know that I should get back to work instead of surfing the web

  1. When I have youtube, facebook and instagram all open at once
  2. When my friend doesn't have work (on any given day that he doesn't) so he sends me reels from social media platforms
  3. When I go down a rabbit hole about some conspiracy theory
  4. When I watch / listen to a podcast that is far more interesting than my work
  5. When I am researching something really quick, but then I see a pop-up add for something that I was searching before and I inevitably click on (because the internet is always listening)
  6. When I am am playing a video-game and can't go to bed unless I win at least one match (but I'm not very good, so)
  7. When I absolutely have to double check where my Amazon package is, even though it wouldn't make any difference on when it actually gets here
  8. When I can't find the perfect song to work to so I end up on youtube for the next half hour (it happens to all of us)
  9. When I get hungry so I google recipes
  10. When I get a random idea to build something that would actually be really cool, so I have to write it down, then inevitably start researching
